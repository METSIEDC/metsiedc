About IEDC

Driving innovation from campus to community.

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) at Met's School of Engineering is a flagship initiative of Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among students and faculty.

We provide a platform for students to pursue entrepreneurial ventures, develop innovative products, and transform their ideas into viable businesses. Our focus is on creating a vibrant ecosystem that supports every stage of the startup journey.

About MET'S IEDC

Fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship, guided by Kerala Startup Mission.

Our Mission

To create an entrepreneurial culture in our institution and to foster growth of innovation and entrepreneurship amongst the students, faculty, and staff. We aim to provide a platform for the students to pursue entrepreneurial activities and to translate their innovative ideas into commercially viable products or services.

Our Vision

To be a leading center for innovation and entrepreneurship development, nurturing successful startups that contribute to economic growth and societal impact, in line with the objectives of Kerala Startup Mission.

Our Association with Kerala Startup Mission

Our IEDC operates under the guidance and support of the Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM), the central agency of the Government of Kerala for entrepreneurship development and incubation activities in Kerala. KSUM's initiatives are aimed at transforming Kerala into a vibrant startup hub. We actively participate in KSUM programs, leverage their network, and align our activities with their strategic goals for fostering innovation in the state.

Our Initiatives

Programs and support systems designed to nurture student innovation.

Idea Incubation

We provide mentorship, resources, and seed funding opportunities to transform promising ideas into viable startup projects.

Skill Development Workshops

Regular workshops on cutting-edge technologies, business management, marketing, and intellectual property rights.

Mentor Connect Program

Connecting student entrepreneurs with experienced industry mentors, successful founders, and subject matter experts.

Industry Linkages

Facilitating collaborations with industry partners for internships, projects, and technology transfer opportunities.

Pitching Platforms

Organizing internal and participating in external pitching competitions to help students refine their ideas and gain exposure.

Pre-Incubation Support

Providing early-stage support for student ventures, helping them develop prototypes and business models.
