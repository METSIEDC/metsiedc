About MET'S School of Engineering

MET'S School of Engineering stands as a beacon of technical excellence and innovation under the Mala Education Trust. Established in 2002, approved by AICTE and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, the institution has spent over two decades fostering a culture of research-driven engineering. With specialized expertise in Biotechnology, CSE, AI & Data Science, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering, MET'S provides the ideal ecosystem for this State-Level Buildathon by bridging the gap between classroom theory and industrial application. MET'S serves as a premier launchpad where student ingenuity meets state-of-the-art infrastructure to solve real-world challenges.
