ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Entrepreneurship Awareness Workshop: A Collaborative Success

On August 22, 2025, the IEDC METS School of Engineering, in collaboration with the Ministry of MSME, Govt. of India, successfully hosted an Entrepreneurship Awareness Workshop at the college's Seminar Hall. The event was attended by 127 participants and featured addresses from key figures from both the college and the ministry, providing aspiring student innovators with valuable insights into the world of entrepreneurship.

IEDC Orientation for B.Tech Students

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) recently conducted an orientation session for B.Tech students from the second, third, and fourth years. The session was aimed at introducing the students to the various initiatives and opportunities offered by IEDC, with a focus on fostering an entrepreneurial mindset. The event provided a platform for students to learn how they can transform their innovative ideas into viable projects and startups with the support of the IEDC.
