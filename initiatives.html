Programs & Support

Our Initiatives

Fostering innovation through structured programs designed to nurture student startups and build the next generation of tech leaders.

Idea Incubation

We provide mentorship, resources, and seed funding opportunities to transform promising ideas into viable startup projects.

Skill Workshops

Regular workshops on cutting-edge technologies, business management, marketing, and intellectual property rights.

Mentor Connect

Connecting student entrepreneurs with experienced industry mentors, successful founders, and subject matter experts.

Industry Linkages

Facilitating collaborations with industry partners for internships, projects, and technology transfer opportunities.

Pitching Platforms

Organizing internal and participating in external pitching competitions to help students refine their ideas and gain exposure.

Pre-Incubation

Providing early-stage support for student ventures, helping them develop prototypes and scalable business models.

MET'S IEDC.

Fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students at MET'S School of Engineering. Supported by Kerala Startup Mission.
